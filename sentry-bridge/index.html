FAQ

Does Sentry Bridge work on the free / Team Sentry plan?

Yes, as long as your plan can send the issue webhook (available on every Sentry plan). That webhook is what the official app reserves auto-creation for on paid tiers — Sentry Bridge acts on it directly.

Will it include the stack trace in the Jira issue?

Honest answer: it depends on your Sentry plan. The issue.created webhook every plan can use carries the culprit, level, environment, event count and permalink — but not the exception or stack frames. If your plan lets you subscribe to the Errors webhook resource, the exception type and top frames are included too. Either way the "Open in Sentry" link takes you straight to the full trace.

Will I get a flood of duplicate tickets?

No. One Sentry issue maps to one Jira issue for the life of the installation. The same error firing repeatedly updates the one issue instead of creating new ones.

Does any of my data leave Atlassian?

No. Sentry Bridge is a Forge app with no egress permissions in its manifest — it makes zero outbound calls. It stores only your settings and a Sentry-issue → Jira-issue mapping, inside your own Atlassian tenant. Verify the permissions at install.
